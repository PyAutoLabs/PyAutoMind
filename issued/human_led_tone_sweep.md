# Human-led tone sweep across PyAutoScientist repos and docs

Filed 2026-07-10. Queued from the org-profile rewrite session
(PyAutoLabs/.github PR #2), where the maintainer set the new framing.

## Original request (verbatim, from the org-README session)

> Instead of framing this as "Open-source astronomy software, developed by an
> AI scientist.", instead make the focus on this being something liek "An
> agentic AI software development ecosystem for human-led natural language
> software development", the focus is on how the human is the lead and its
> all about enabling natalural to lead ther work. Maybe make sure this tone
> permeates through the PyAutoscientist repos, docs

## Settled framing (approved on the org front page — reuse, don't reinvent)

- Tagline: **"An agentic AI ecosystem for human-led, natural-language
  software development."**
- Intro voice: "You describe what you want in plain English; an organism of
  AI agents plans, builds, tests and releases it — with you making every
  judgment call."
- The human is the lead; agents are the labour. "Fork it and lead your own"
  rather than "run your own". Mind is "where you lead".

## Scope

Sweep the human-led, natural-language-first tone through:

1. `PyAutoBrain/ORGANISM.md` — the canonical organism page (edit here first;
   everything else links to it).
2. The six organ READMEs: PyAutoBrain, PyAutoMind, PyAutoHeart, PyAutoBuild,
   PyAutoMemory, PyAutoConf (Nerves).
3. The PyAutoScientist RTD docs (`PyAutoBrain/docs/`): `index.md`, the
   `concepts/` pages, and the adoption guide — e.g. index currently says
   "with a human checkpointing the judgment calls", which is passive; the
   human should read as the one driving.

Tone changes only — no boundary/contract changes; ORGANISM.md's organ
boundaries and the call chain stay as-is. Keep it light: rewording of
taglines, intro paragraphs and framing sentences, not wholesale rewrites.

## Notes

- The org front page (PyAutoLabs/.github `profile/README.md`) already
  carries the framing after PR #2 — use it as the reference voice.
- Consistent with the existing user-docs stance: agentic AI is first-class,
  assistants are "point Claude/ChatGPT at it and ask".
